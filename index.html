How does it work? ▾
The extension captures the content from your current browser tab, cleans and formats it, then opens a beautiful preview page. Click "Save as PDF" to export using your browser's built-in PDF printer. Everything runs locally — nothing is uploaded anywhere.
Is my data private? ▾
Yes, 100%. The extension processes everything locally in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit your content. No analytics, no tracking, no data collection.
Which AI platforms are supported? ▾
ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, and essentially any website. You can select any text on any page and export it.
How do I activate my Pro license? ▾
After purchasing on Gumroad, you'll receive a license key via email. Open the extension, go to Settings, paste your key, and click Activate. Pro features unlock instantly.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▾
Yes. Cancel anytime from your Gumroad account. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
Does it work on Firefox or Edge? ▾
Currently Chrome only. Edge support (same extension) works by side-loading. Firefox version is on our roadmap.
