🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AI to PDF and its affiliates ("AI to PDF", "we", "our" or "us") collect, use, and share information in connection with your use of our websites (including https://aitopdf.com), Chrome extension, services, and applications (collectively, the "Services").

We may collect and receive information about users of our Services ("users", "you" or "your") from various sources. We recommend reading this Privacy Policy in full. For questions, contact us at support@aitopdf.com.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Payment Information

When you purchase AI to PDF Pro, payment is processed by Gumroad. We do not store your financial information, but retain subscriber info (email, license key) via Gumroad.

1.2. Cookies and Tracking

We gather certain information automatically such as IP addresses, browser type, referring/exit pages, and clickstream data via cookies and log files to improve our Services.

1.3. Conversation Content

We do not store or analyze your conversation content. For all export formats (PDF, DOCX, Markdown), processing is completed entirely locally in your browser. Your content never leaves your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Services
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our Services
  • Communicate with you for customer service and updates
  • Process your transactions and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal requirements

3. How We Share Information

We may share information with vendors and service providers, analytics providers (Google Analytics, Mixpanel), and as required by law. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Chrome Extension Permissions

Permission Why We Need It
activeTab To capture content from the page you're viewing when you click Export.
scripting To extract chat conversation HTML from AI platforms for PDF conversion.
storage To save your license key and export count locally. Never sent to us.
contextMenus To add the right-click "Export to PDF" option.
host_permissions — api.gumroad.com To verify Pro license keys. Only the license key is transmitted.

5. Data Retention

  • Temporary export data is overwritten with each new export
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data
  • Monthly export counts reset automatically each calendar month

6. GDPR Rights

If you are in the EEA, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Contact support@aitopdf.com to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

AI to PDF does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Contact us at support@aitopdf.com if you believe a child has provided personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website. Continued use of the Services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
