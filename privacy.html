🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

What AI to PDF Does

AI to PDF is a Chrome extension that captures AI chat conversations from your browser and converts them into beautifully formatted PDF documents. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data We Collect

❌ Data we DO NOT collect

  • We do NOT read, store, or transmit your conversations
  • We do NOT collect browsing history
  • We do NOT track which websites you visit
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking scripts
  • We do NOT sell any data to third parties
  • We do NOT use cookies

✅ Data processed locally (never leaves your browser)

  • Page content is captured only when you click the export button
  • Captured content is stored temporarily in local browser storage
  • Content is used solely to generate your PDF
  • No data is sent to our servers — we don't have servers

⚠️ Data sent to third parties

Gumroad (payment processor): If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is verified against Gumroad's API (api.gumroad.com). Only the license key is sent — no conversation data, no browsing data, no personal information from the extension.

See Gumroad's Privacy Policy.

Permissions Explained

We request the minimum permissions necessary. Here's exactly what each one does:

Permission Why We Need It
activeTab To capture content from the page you're viewing when you click Export. Only activates on the current tab, only when you click.
scripting To extract the chat conversation HTML from AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) for PDF conversion.
storage To save your license key and monthly export count locally in your browser. Synced via your Chrome profile, never sent to us.
contextMenus To add the right-click "Export to PDF" option for quick access.
host_permissions
api.gumroad.com 		To verify Pro license keys against Gumroad's payment API. Only the license key is transmitted — nothing else.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • License key — synced across your Chrome browsers via your Google account (Chrome Sync)
  • Monthly export count — stored locally only, resets each month
  • Temporary export data — stored locally, overwritten with each new export

We have no servers, no databases, and no backend. We literally cannot access your data.

Data Retention

  • Temporary export data is overwritten each time you create a new export
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data automatically
  • Your license key remains in Chrome Sync until you deactivate it or uninstall

Children's Privacy

AI to PDF is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Delete all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Deactivate your license from the extension's Settings tab
  • Clear export data via Chrome's "Clear browsing data" → Extensions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
